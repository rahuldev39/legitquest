Bio

Karan completed his law degree from Faculty of Law, Delhi University. He has worked as a Senior Associate with Mr. Ram Jethmalani in New Delhi. He studied a Master of Laws (L.L.M.) and the Wharton Business and Law Program from the University of Pennsylvania, U.S.A. in 2016 and came back to India to transform the way we perceive the Indian legal system. By devising a platform that readily brings the law fraternity together with forums, blogs, online library for law students and updates on the latest amendments, he dreams of making legal search and analysis hassle free.
