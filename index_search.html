LegitQuest is world’s only legal research product that provide insight and 1-click access to the segregated sections of the judgment. Our 1-click iDRAF enables researcher to see issue, arguments, reasoning, decision with just one click on mouse.

Search

Not all search engines are created equal, Lawyers need customised search portals which are based on legal propositions

For years, lawyers have been stuck with antiquated tools that focus primarily on Boolean search. Better tools are needed to truly understand data.

iSearch gives you a refreshing outcome by providing relevant results (results you are actually interested), in uncluttered, easy to read interface and helpful options to broaden or tighten a search. The auto complete feature suggests the most relevant document from the Statutes, Case Laws, Regulations and Templates based on legal taxonomy for most efficient, relevant and accurate results.

Graphics

A picture is intelligence made visible

Instead of wading through piles of paper, lawyers now deal with terabytes of data and hundreds of thousands of documents.

Experts have proven that visuals have a positive impact on the human mind for learning and memorising.

iGraphics saves your time by condensing the treatment of overruled, distinguished, followed and relied case laws in important tables, charts and data into a single canvas for easy reference and understanding, thus making it convenient for you to understand the current standing of the case law in hand.

DRAF

Deliver beyond the expectations. World’s first judgment segregation System.

iDRAF stands for “Decision Reasoning Arguments Findings” of a Case Law. It is a Spartan search portal with subtleties that sets it apart from the existing search engines. At LegistQuest, a team of highly experienced Attorneys and Engineers have come together to develop a specialised feature that enables you to locate the iDRAF of a case within seconds. It’s a unique reliable & user friendly tool which recognises your needs and has been created keeping your time constraints in mind.

The feature uses the algorithm to find out the iDRAF of every case at the click of a button. This contains tabs that take you to required portions of the judgment readily.
