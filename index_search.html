Our brain has a hard time processing unstructured and complex data. DRAF

iDRAF stands for “Decision Reasoning Arguments Findings” of a Case Law. Using Artificial Intelligence, a team of highly experienced Attorneys and Engineers at LegitQuest has developed a specialised feature that enables you to find the iDRAF of a case within seconds.

Every time you open a Case Law, the algorithm culls out the iDRAF of that case. This search tool contains tabs that take you to specific portions of the judgment within a few seconds.

DRAF is a great tool to begin a research project, giving you a sense of what kind of material can be found on your topic. It has been thoroughly checked for quality, reliability and utility to legal researchers.
