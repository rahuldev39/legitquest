Legit Quest’s Ambassador Programme

LQ’s CAP is a programme which will empower the young law students to implement the strategies for promoting the Legit Quest’s products on their campus and other approachable vicinity. Campus ambassadors will be the face and voice of Legit Quest at their college/university. Legit Quest is inviting such students to become their official representatives.

What you get?
  • Access to Legit Quest’s product
  • Certificate of appreciation for the association with the organisation from the Patron, Mr. Ram Jethmalani
  • Networking with like-minded people in your fraternity
  • Opportunity to work in close collaboration with the Team Legit Quest to gain relevant experience
  • Exposure to the real life learning during the journey by being our proponent
What you do?
  • Be the official ambassador and representative of Legit Quest in your campus
  • Promote Legit Quest’s products on your campus and other approachable vicinity
  • Organise and coordinate various activities on your campus
  • Update us on the activities undertaken on a monthly basis
Eligibility:
  • Law students who are in their third or fourth year of 5 year integrated LLB program
  • Law students who are in their second year of 3 year LLB program
  • Law students who have leadership qualities
  • Law students who have strong communication skills
