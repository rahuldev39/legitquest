Taking tedious away from legal search

Analytic

LegitQuest is the intersection of Legal Research and Technology. We use advanced technologies such as machine learning and natural language processing to clean up, structure and analyse raw data. It gives you benefit over opposing counsel by providing data-driven insights into how the cases are treated and paragraphs are quoted in the latest judgments. Lawyers can build litigation strategies based on that information.

Comprehensive database

Our portal contains a wealth of primary and secondary database. It features a searchable and browsable database of Supreme Court decisions along with those of the various High Courts, Tribunals and Commissions. These are intelligently linked to secondary sources such as legal blogs, books and articles among others. We have a plethora of data in the form of various documents, case laws, dockets and articles.

Flexible pricing

We understand that you are well informed when it comes to the selection of sources for your legal research and brief preparation. With no predefined packages, our goal is to provide you with hassle-free pricing. Here, you are free to choose content, features and duration as per your requirements.

Artificial Intelligence in Legal Research

Artificial Intelligence Power Legal Research

Legitquest is where Artificial intellegince meets Legal research. We have spent thousands of man days of top legal researchers to find the ways lawyers research could be refined. The input has then gone into our machine learning algorithm to come out with a search which is narrowed down to the most relevant search. We take pride that when you search, you will get the requisite judgment with the most important areas highlighted within the first 5 results, not pages and pages of judgment listings.

This mens weeks of research time will get consolidated into hours, saving both time and money for yourself and your clients.
